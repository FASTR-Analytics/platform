# FASTR Analytics Platform - Video Outline (2-3 min)

## Section 1: What is FASTR? (15-20 sec)

- Health data analytics platform built for country health teams
- Manages facility-level health data (HMIS) and health facility assessments (HFA)
- Used to turn raw data into actionable visualizations, reports, and insights

## Section 2: Quick Platform Tour (40-50 sec)

- **Data import**: Upload HMIS/HFA datasets with multi-step validation, or pull directly from DHIS2
- **Indicators & structure**: Define health indicators, administrative areas, facility types
- **Processing modules**: Automated R-based data processing pipelines — configure parameters, hit run, results update downstream
- **Visualizations**: Charts, maps, tables — filter by period, disaggregation, admin level
- **Reports & export**: Compose multi-visualization reports, export to PDF/PPT/DOCX
- **Multi-language**: Full English/French support

## Section 3: AI Features — Overview (20-30 sec)

- AI assistant panel built into the platform (right side chat)
- Powered by Claude — context-aware, knows your project's data, indicators, and structure
- Not a generic chatbot: has tools to directly read and modify your project content

## Section 4: AI Features — Deep Dive (50-70 sec)

- **Context awareness**: AI adapts based on what you're doing — browsing visualizations vs editing a slide deck vs reviewing metrics
- **Slide deck creation**: AI can draft slides, insert visualizations, modify layouts — with live preview before saving
- **Visualization configuration**: AI helps configure charts, select disaggregations, tailor presentations to the data
- **Metric intelligence**: AI has access to indicator metadata — descriptions, methodology, typical ranges — and can explain what you're seeing
- **Reference documents**: Upload methodology docs or guidelines; AI analyzes them and provides contextual advice grounded in your materials
- **Saved prompts**: Store prompt templates for recurring analytical tasks

## Section 5: Wrap-up (10-15 sec)

- AI is deeply integrated — not bolted on, but woven into the workflow
- Goal: help health teams spend less time wrangling data and more time acting on it
